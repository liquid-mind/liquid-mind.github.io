About

Liquid mind is the container for a number of projects that began at the ETH in Zurich, Switzerland and are currently being further developed at the Zurich University of Applied Sciences in Winterthur.

The goal is to provide high-level abstractions for the Java programming language that facilitate building and maintaining complex systems in the enterprise as well as bridging the gap between programming and visual modelling.
